The Applied Psychology Podcast is hosted by Dan Cox and features conversations with the world's foremost applied psychology experts. We cover a variety of applied psychology topics such as psychotherapy, mental health, happiness, interpersonal relationships, and much more.

“Welcome to The Applied Psychology Podcast. I'm your host, Dan Cox, and I'm a professor in counseling psychology at the University of British Columbia. In our inaugural season, we're going to be talking about all sorts of applied psychology topics, from psychotherapy to mental health, to happiness, to interpersonal relationships, with the world's foremost experts.So join me and we'll learn some interesting stuff, meet some interesting people, and hopefully I won't screw up too badly in the first season of The Applied Psychology Podcast.”
