Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the podcast, episodes, and how to get in touch.

How often are new episodes released?

New episodes are released regularly. Subscribe to the podcast to get notified whenever a new episode drops. Check back on our episodes page for the latest releases.

Can I suggest a guest or topic?

Absolutely! We'd love to hear your suggestions. Send us a message with your ideas, or record a voice message via Speakpipe.

Where can I find transcripts?

Transcripts are available directly on each episode's page on this website. Head to the Episodes page, open any episode, and look for the Transcript tab alongside the show notes and chapters.

Can I share episodes?

Yes! We encourage you to share episodes with colleagues, friends, and anyone who might find the content valuable. The easiest way is to visit the individual page for any episode — you'll find share links right there. You can also find all episodes on the major platforms below.

Apple Podcasts Spotify YouTube Pocket Casts

How can I contact Dan?

You can send a message via the contact page, connect with Dan on LinkedIn, or follow him on Bluesky.

Are there opportunities to collaborate?

We're always interested in collaboration opportunities, whether it's guest appearances, research partnerships, or other creative projects. Please get in touch to discuss potential collaborations.
