About the Podcast

Psychotherapy and Applied Psychology brings together cutting-edge research with practical applications. Each episode features in-depth conversations with leading experts across psychology, neuroscience, cognitive science, and behavioral research.

Whether you're a mental health professional, student, researcher, or someone interested in understanding human behavior, this podcast provides valuable insights and evidence-based perspectives on the topics that matter most.

Join Dan as we explore the intersections of theory and practice in applied psychology.
